Program

Learn New Things

Maker Fest aims to show that technology is not intimidating. This Festival is the place where we will show that anyone, regardless of age, tech know-how and field of interest can all be together for technology. In this event, passionate makers will be gathered to show-off their projects and ideas where exhibitors and attendees alike can learn from.

Explore New Possibilities

Show-off your work as an exhibitor and get feedback from fellow exhibitors and Festival attendees. Explore possibilities of collaborating with other Makers and get support from communities and organizations. As an attendee, you get to learn from makers and explore cool projects proudly Pinoy made.

Collaborate and Connect

Meet other makers, discuss your ideas and start turning them into reality. Connect with possible partner communities and get a chance to be discovered by companies and organizations.

Know More About The Community

Get to know more people from the Mozilla Philippines Community and be part of the growing community of web champions and protectors.

What is a 'maker'?

Simply, anyone who makes something! It is a term not popularly used in the Philippines to identify people who create awesome stuff as we like calling them 'inventors', 'engineers' and so on. By using the term â€˜makerâ€™ we are opening the Festival to all kinds of people from various fields who are using the Web, making for the Web or would like to explore the possibilities of using the help of the Web for the things they are making.

What projects are allowed in the Festival?

Anything related to technology and the use of technology to make lives easier and more meaningful. Some of the projects we are looking for are related to:

  • Web development
  • Mobile app development
  • Software development
  • Open source projects
  • Robotics
  • Web apps
  • Web services
  • Arts and crafts using the help of technology (i.e. using a blog etc.)
  • Technology solutions to education
  • Digital media learning solutions
  • Innovative hardware projects
  • Tech projects for non-profit
  • Tech projects for public service
  • Computer games
